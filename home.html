Nigeria is among the top five nations in the world of out-of-school children of primary and secondary school age. Even more, 34% of children drop out of school before upper-primary school. Due to a lack of access, affordability, and awareness, children in rural areas are unable to receive the education to escape poverty. Helpers Social Development Foundation is going the mile to fulfill our mission as a charity and opening a nursery and primary school! This project is meant to benefit families that live in the remote area of Oji River. The locality doesn’t have piped water or electricity, so families don’t usually prioritize education. However, we at HSDF know that in every underprivileged child there lies the potential to finally break the cycle of poverty.
